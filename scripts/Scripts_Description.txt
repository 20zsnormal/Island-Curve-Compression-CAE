***************************
Data Preprocessing
Segmentation：Divide the line data into segments of equal distance.
Resampling：Resampling the segmented arc segments ensures that the input data has the same length.

****************************
Model train and estimate

train_gpu: Training neural networks.

estimate: Evaluating the accuracy of neural networks.

estimate2.py: Supplementary Metric Evaluation. This script extends the evaluation to high-order geometric fidelity. 
It calculates additional metrics such as Relative Area Error (RAE) and Relative Perimeter Error (RPE) to ensure the morphological integrity of the compressed data.

fourier_estimate: Evaluating the accuracy of the Fourier Series Method.

fourier_estimate2.py: Performs additional geometric metric calculations for the Fourier method to ensure a fair and comprehensive comparison with the proposed deep learning model.

Douglas-Peucker.py: Classical Algorithm Evaluation. This script evaluates the Douglas-Peucker (DP) simplification algorithm. 
It allows for a direct comparison between our deep learning-based approach and the classical geometry-based simplification standard in GIS.

DP_estimate.py: DP Metric Evaluation. Conducts a detailed metric assessment of the curves simplified by the DP algorithm, measuring its performance in preserving geometric characteristics.

****************************
Data restore
txt2shp：Convert the results of neural network training back into vector data.
